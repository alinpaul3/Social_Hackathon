HackThemes

For SH 2025, we've identified these critical areas where technology can drive meaningful social change. Teams may choose to work within one of these themes or propose an innovative solution that addresses a social challenge not covered here.

I

Student Innovation using IndiaStack

Leverage IndiaStack's digital infrastructure (Aadhaar, UPI, DigiLocker, etc.) to create innovative solutions that enhance governance, finance, and public services.

Example Challenges:

  • Digital identity verification for seamless public services
  • Financial inclusion platforms using UPI integration
  • Document management solutions with DigiLocker
  • Privacy-preserving applications using IndiaStack APIs
U

Urban Planning

Design smart, sustainable, and tech-driven urban solutions to optimize transportation, housing, and public infrastructure for better city living.

Example Challenges:

  • Traffic optimization systems for congested urban areas
  • Smart housing solutions for urban density challenges
  • Public space utilization and management tools
  • Resource allocation systems for urban infrastructure
E

Education

Develop AI-driven, personalized, and accessible learning platforms to improve education quality, inclusivity, and skill development.

Example Challenges:

  • Adaptive learning systems for diverse student needs
  • Low-resource educational tools for underserved communities
  • AI-powered skill assessment and career guidance platforms
  • Collaboration tools for distributed learning environments
G

Energy Efficiency in AI/Computing

Optimize AI models and computing systems to reduce energy consumption, improve sustainability, and lower carbon footprints.

Example Challenges:

  • Lightweight AI model design for mobile applications
  • Energy monitoring and optimization tools for data centers
  • Efficient edge computing solutions for IoT devices
  • Carbon footprint calculators for computational workloads
H

Healthcare Access

Create solutions that improve healthcare accessibility, particularly for underserved communities, through telemedicine, AI diagnostics, and digital health records.

Example Challenges:

  • Telemedicine platforms for remote diagnostics
  • AI-powered health screening tools for resource-limited settings
  • Medical record systems for continuity of care
  • Health education and preventive care applications
F

Financial Inclusion

Build tools and platforms that extend financial services to unbanked and underbanked populations, focusing on security, accessibility, and education.

Example Challenges:

  • Simplified banking interfaces for first-time users
  • Peer-to-peer lending platforms for underserved communities
  • Financial literacy education tools and simulators
  • Offline-capable payment solutions for areas with limited connectivity
C

Climate Action Tech

Develop monitoring tools, renewable energy solutions, and carbon footprint trackers to combat climate change and promote environmental sustainability.

Example Challenges:

  • Environmental data collection and visualization platforms
  • Community-based renewable energy management systems
  • Personal and organizational carbon footprint trackers
  • AI-powered resource consumption optimization tools

Cross-theme Innovation

We encourage participants to consider solutions that span multiple themes. Some of the most impactful innovations emerge at the intersection of different domains. Teams are welcome to combine elements from different themes to create comprehensive solutions for complex social challenges.
