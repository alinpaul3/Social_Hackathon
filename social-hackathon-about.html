About

Our Mission

The Social Hackathon brings together tech innovators, developers, and changemakers to create technology-driven solutions for pressing social challenges. We believe in the power of collaborative innovation to address global issues and create meaningful impact.

Founded in 2019, our hackathon has grown into a dynamic space for social innovation, bringing together tech enthusiasts and problem-solvers. We focus on real-world challenges, sustainable development goals, and creating practical, accessible technology solutions to support communities.

The 2025 edition will run for 24 hours of intense collaboration, mentorship, and innovation from May 15-16, 2025, bringing together diverse perspectives and expertise.
